Rígin

This is what I choosen for my graded unit in college.

Rí is a Gaelic word for king. My main intention was to create a label that express the meaning of the word. I used monograms because monograms are where used in the past by kings and other royal figures. monograms nowadays are considered quite as distinctive and people use it as logo for themselves(me included!)

Logo

A lot of thought was put in this logo.

I wanted something that suggest dominance which is why the "í" is significantly smaller that "R" to show the contrast in size.
